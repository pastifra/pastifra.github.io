Biography

Hi, I'm Francesco Pasti!.

I pursued a Bachelor's in Electronics Engineering and continued my education with a Master's in Control Systems Engineering.

After my Mater's I worked for one year at Amazon as an Automation Engineer before deciding to go back to Academia.

Right now I'm pursuing a PhD in the National Doctorate "Robotics and Intelligent Machines".

I am based at the University of Padova with Prof. Nicola Bellotto as my direct supervisor.

Research Interests

I am currently working on Active Perception, Tiny AI and Computer Vision in the Robotics domain.

I am interested in researching how AI algorithms can be optimized to meet the computational constraints of Embedded Systems.
